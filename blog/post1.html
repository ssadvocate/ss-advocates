5 Steps to File an FIR in Maharashtra

Published on: August 1, 2025

Filing an FIR is the first step towards seeking justice. Here are 5 key steps to file an FIR in Maharashtra:

  1. Visit your nearest police station.
  2. Explain the incident clearly and truthfully.
  3. Provide supporting documents (if any).
  4. Ensure the FIR is written correctly — ask for a copy.
  5. Follow up with the investigation officer.
